The ULX3S FPGA Educational Development board with the onboard ESP32 and various peripherals is one of the most flexible and versatile boards on the market.



There are many examples to get started at ulx3s.github.io.

The ULX3S is available from Mouser and Crowd Supply as well as Lectronz and Envox Experimental Zone.

Beware of 3rd party sales that may not have been as exhaustively tested and confirmed operational. Saving a few bucks in exchange for endless hours of troublshooting may not be a wise choice. The original developers also thank for for your support in buying Genuine Radiona boards from one of the above sellers.

Of course, you are always welcome to make your own board! See kitspace. The source is found on github.com/emard/ulx3s.

There's an open source custom enclosure for the Elecrow 7" HDMI display available at github.com/gojimmypi/ulx3s-elecrow-7inch-hdmi-enclosure.

See the blog update.
